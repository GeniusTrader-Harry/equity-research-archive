The call is a mispriced regulatory overhang. The market prices a severe SAMR antitrust outcome onto a three-year-low multiple, but precedent (Alibaba, Meituan) says the most likely remedy is absorbable — so on any non-severe ruling the multiple re-rates from ~12.4× toward its own 15–18× history. Two operating pillars — international margin inflection and a durable China core — underwrite the base while that re-rate plays out. The base case prices the expected −10% commission remedy, sitting ~4–10% below consensus by design; bull $86 (+85%) / bear $32 (−31%), skew 2.8×. The master tripwire (KC5) is a structural SAMR commission cap.
